Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 2025-02-12

Welcome to Parking Sign Master (Sweden), an application provided by Sparkling AI AB, a company registered in Sweden ("Company," "we," "us," or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use the Parking Sign Master (Sweden) app ("App"). By using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Data We Collect

We collect the following types of data when you use the App:

a) Contact Information

  • Name (Used for App Functionality)
  • Email Address (Used for App Functionality)

b) Usage Data

  • Product Interaction (Used for Analytics & App Functionality)

c) Diagnostics Data

  • Crash Data (Used for Analytics & App Functionality)

d) Uploaded Data

  • Images of Parking Signs (Used for AI model improvement, feature testing, and bug fixes; stored temporarily for up to 12 months)

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data for the following purposes:

  • To provide core functionality of the App, including processing parking sign images.
  • To improve AI models by using uploaded images to enhance sign recognition.
  • To process in-app purchases and manage credit-based transactions.
  • To analyze user behavior and enhance user experience via Firebase Analytics.
  • To handle user authentication and account management via Clerk.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions via RevenueCat.
  • To ensure app stability by collecting crash data and diagnosing technical issues.
  • To comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse (e.g., preventing exploitation of free credits).

3. Data Sharing & Third-Party Services

We do not sell your data to third parties. However, we use third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Azure OpenAI API (for AI-based recognition and processing)
  • Google APIs (for authentication and location-based services)
  • Firebase Analytics (for app usage tracking and diagnostics)
  • Clerk (for user authentication and account management)
  • RevenueCat (for in-app purchases and subscription management)

These third-party providers process data according to their own privacy policies.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • All data is stored and processed within the European Union (EU) to comply with GDPR.
  • Uploaded images are stored temporarily for up to 12 months and then automatically deleted.
  • User account data is retained until the account is deleted or terminated.
  • Transaction/payment history is kept until the user account is deleted or terminated.

5. User Rights (GDPR Compliance)

As a user in the EU, you have the following rights under GDPR:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your stored personal data.
  • Right to Correction: Update incorrect or incomplete data.
  • Right to Deletion: Delete your account via the app, which will erase all associated data.
  • Right to Object: You cannot opt out of AI model training or analytics, as they are essential to the App's functionality.
  • Right to Data Portability: Request your data in a structured format.

To exercise these rights, contact us at app-support@sparkling-ai.com.

6. Data Security Measures

We take the security of your data seriously. Measures include:

  • Encryption of stored data to prevent unauthorized access.
  • Secure authentication for user accounts via Clerk.
  • Restricted data access for authorized personnel only.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is intended for users aged 15 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 15.

8. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. The latest version will always be available in the App. Continued use of the App after changes means you accept the updated policy.
